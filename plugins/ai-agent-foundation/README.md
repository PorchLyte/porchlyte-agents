# AI Agent Foundation

The three skills every AI Agent Team member reads from. Set these up first.

## What's inside

- **Voice** — How you sound. Pulls out your tone, your phrases, the agent-speak you hate, your signature moves.
- **Brand** — How you look and feel. Colors, fonts, aesthetic direction, what your brand is and what it isn't.
- **Local** — Your market. The neighborhoods, the locals-only knowledge, the quirks, the real dynamic on the ground.

## How to install

1. Open Claude on your desktop (the Claude desktop app, with Cowork enabled).
2. In the sidebar, click **Customize > Plugins > Add > Upload plugin**.
3. Select the `ai-agent-foundation.zip` file you downloaded.
4. Once installed, type `/foundations-setup` in a Cowork chat to begin.

The setup walks you through Voice, Brand, and Local one at a time. Plan on 15 to 20 minutes if you do all three back to back. You can also stop after one and come back later. Your work saves.

## After setup

Every other AI Agent plugin will read from these three skills automatically. You don't need to do anything to connect them. The Foundations show up as available context the moment any other AI Agent skill runs.
